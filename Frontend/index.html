Process
How It Works
A transparent, step-by-step intelligence pipeline from raw data to ranked decisions.

Data Aggregation

Concurrent API calls fetch country profiles, live weather, AQI, healthcare stats, and travel advisories from independent public sources.

Normalization

All heterogeneous metrics are normalized to a 0–100 scale ensuring fair, comparable scoring across countries with different data ranges.

Score Computation

Three intelligence scores computed per country — Travel Risk, Health Infrastructure, and Environmental Stability.

Dynamic Weighting

Risk tolerance and duration of stay dynamically shift scoring weights — prioritizing what matters most for your context.

Ranked Results

Countries ranked most to least suitable with a composite score and structured reasoning citing key contributing factors.

Smart Caching

Results cached 60 minutes with cache-hit deduplication. Subsequent analyses of cached countries respond instantly.
